# TechCorp Vacation and Time Off Policy

## Unlimited PTO (Paid Time Off)

TechCorp offers an unlimited PTO policy, trusting employees to manage their time responsibly.

### Guidelines

- **Minimum Recommended**: 3 weeks (15 days) per year
- **Manager Approval**: Required for all time off requests
- **Advance Notice**: 
  - 2 weeks notice for 1-3 days off
  - 1 month notice for 1+ weeks off
- **Blackout Periods**: Limited time off during Q4 product launches

## Company Holidays

TechCorp observes 10 paid holidays per year (US calendar):
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Day after Thanksgiving
- Christmas Eve
- Christmas Day

## Winter Break

**Office Closure**: December 24-31

The entire company shuts down for the last week of the year. This does not count against your PTO.

## Summer Fridays

**Period**: June through August

**Policy**: Half-day Fridays (work until 1 PM)

Enjoy your summer with early Friday finishes. Perfect for weekend getaways!

## Sick Leave

- Unlimited sick days (separate from PTO)
- No doctor's note required for 1-3 days
- Extended illness: Work with HR for leave options

## Best Practices

- Coordinate with your team before taking time off
- Set up auto-responders and delegate work
- Truly disconnect - we encourage work-life balance!
- Don't let PTO accumulate unused - take time to recharge
