# TechCorp Remote Work Policy (2024)

## Policy Overview

Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week.

## Requirements

- **Core Hours**: 10 AM - 3 PM local time (must be available)
- **Team Meetings**: Must attend in-person on Wednesdays
- **VPN Access**: Required for all remote connections
- **Remote Work Stipend**: $500/year for home office equipment

## Approval Process

Remote work arrangements must be approved by your direct manager. Submit requests at least 24 hours in advance through the HR portal.

## Equipment

The company provides:
- Laptop with required software
- VPN access credentials
- Video conferencing tools

Employees are responsible for:
- Internet connection
- Home office setup
- Ergonomic furniture (stipend available)
