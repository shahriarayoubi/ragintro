# TechCorp Health Benefits

## Medical Insurance

- **Coverage**: Employee and dependents
- **Deductible**: $0 for in-network providers
- **Premium**: Fully covered by TechCorp

## Dental and Vision

- Included at no extra cost to employees
- Network of providers nationwide
- Annual cleanings and eye exams covered 100%

## Mental Health Support

- **Sessions Covered**: 20 sessions per year at 100%
- **Providers**: Licensed therapists and counselors
- **Access**: Through our EAP (Employee Assistance Program)
- **Confidential**: All sessions are completely private

## Wellness Benefits

- **Annual Wellness Credit**: $300 for fitness/wellness activities
- Can be used for:
  - Gym memberships
  - Fitness classes
  - Wellness apps
  - Sports equipment
  - Massage therapy

## Parental Leave

- **Primary Caregiver**: 16 weeks paid leave
- **Secondary Caregiver**: 8 weeks paid leave
- Flexible return-to-work schedule available

## Additional Benefits

- Prescription drug coverage
- Preventive care at 100%
- Telehealth services
- FSA and HSA options available
