# Drama Movies Collection

**Top Drama Films:**

1. **The Shawshank Redemption** (1994)
   - Director: Frank Darabont
   - Rating: 9.3/10
   - Description: Powerful story of hope and friendship in prison spanning decades

2. **Parasite** (2019)
   - Director: Bong Joon-ho
   - Rating: 8.5/10
   - Description: Gripping social commentary about class divide and inequality

3. **Moonlight** (2016)
   - Director: Barry Jenkins
   - Rating: 7.4/10
   - Description: Beautiful coming-of-age story told in three chapters

**Characteristics:**
- Emotional depth and complexity
- Strong character development
- Powerful storytelling
- Meaningful themes
