# Comedy Movies Collection

**Top Comedy Films:**

1. **The Grand Budapest Hotel** (2014)
   - Director: Wes Anderson
   - Rating: 8.1/10
   - Description: Whimsical comedy about a legendary hotel concierge and his protégé

2. **Superbad** (2007)
   - Director: Greg Mottola
   - Rating: 7.6/10
   - Description: Coming-of-age comedy about high school friends on a wild night

3. **Bridesmaids** (2011)
   - Director: Paul Feig
   - Rating: 6.8/10
   - Description: Hilarious wedding comedy with great ensemble cast

- Laugh-out-loud moments
