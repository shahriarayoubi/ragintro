# CloudTech Solutions - Support Policy

## Support Channels

### Email Support
- Available for all plan tiers
- Response time: Within 24 hours
- Email: support@cloudtech-solutions.com

### Chat Support
- Available for Professional and Enterprise plans
- Response time: Within 2 hours
- Available: 24/7

### Phone Support
- Exclusive to Enterprise plan customers
- Direct line to senior engineers
- Response time: Immediate
- Available: 24/7/365

## Support Request Guidelines

1. **Account Verification**: All support requests require account verification
2. **Priority Levels**:
   - Critical: Service outage or data loss (1-hour response)
   - High: Major functionality impaired (4-hour response)
   - Medium: Minor issues affecting operations (24-hour response)
   - Low: General questions or feature requests (48-hour response)
